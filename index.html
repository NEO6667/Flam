Bezier Curve Math

Cubic Bezier Formula:
B(t) = (1-t)³P₀ + 3(1-t)²tP₁ + 3(1-t)t²P₂ + t³P₃

Tangent Formula:
B'(t) = 3(1-t)²(P₁-P₀) + 6(1-t)t(P₂-P₁) + 3t²(P₃-P₂)

Physics Model

Spring-Damping System:
acceleration = -k × (position - target) - damping × velocity

P₀ and P₃ are fixed endpoints
P₁ and P₂ follow mouse with spring physics

How to Interact

  • Move your mouse over the canvas to influence control points P₁ and P₂.
  • Adjust the sliders to change spring physics behavior.
  • Toggle buttons to show/hide visual elements.
  • The curve shows tangent vectors at multiple points (blue lines).
  • Control points have spring physics for natural motion.
